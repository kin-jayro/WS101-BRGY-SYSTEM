System Features & Modules

Discover the integrated data management capabilities and public services offered by our platform.

Public Dashboard

Tracks live community population statistics, hosts real-time urgent announcements, distributes localized news releases, and calendars upcoming neighborhood activities seamlessly.

👥

Barangay Officials Management

Maintains directory profiles of actively serving community officials. Includes tracking systems for distinct leadership roles, active service durations, and contact channels.

ℹ️

Barangay Information

Provides a static, centralized overview of foundational jurisdiction metrics, location data, province listings, hotlines, and official organizational contact points securely.

📋

Surveys

Empowers the community by hosting local polls and feedback questionnaires. Allows administrators to safely gather resident insights on ongoing neighborhood projects and city initiatives.

📁

File Request

Enables digitized processing for community clearances, residency certificates, and permit paperwork to reduce long waiting queues at the physical hall.

📖

Blotter Records Registry

Handles structured data management for community peace, security logs, legal classifications, incident details, and structural status tracking metrics safely.
